Science Defenders

Answer science questions to earn energy & coins, then build towers to stop the invasion before it reaches your lab.

  • Before every wave you must answer 3 science questions — each correct MCQ earns energy & gold.
  • Spend energy to build towers on the glowing stone pads beside the path.
  • Towers auto-fire at enemies in range. Upgrade them with coins for more damage.
  • Survive all waves. Each enemy that reaches the lab costs you Base HP.
