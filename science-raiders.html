👾 Science Raiders

Blast through a procedurally generated science lab gone rogue. Clear every room, then answer a science question to choose a power-up. Die and the run ends — but each correct answer makes your next descent deadlier.

  • Move with WASD / arrow keys. Your blaster auto-fires toward your mouse (or the nearest enemy on touch).
  • Tap Space or Shift to dash through danger (brief invulnerability).
  • Clear a room to trigger a science MCQ. Answer correctly to choose an upgrade — level an element to 5/10, or level up your skill.
  • Pick one skill below — it's the only one you'll wield this run. Fire it with key Q.
